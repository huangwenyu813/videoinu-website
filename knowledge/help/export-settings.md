# Export Settings

## Summary
Export is the final step for turning your working result into a deliverable video file.

## When to use
Use this guide when your output is ready and you want to save or publish the final result.

## Steps
1. Confirm the final result is the version you want to export.
2. Open the export or output settings panel.
3. Choose the output format and resolution that match your delivery target.
4. Review quality-related settings before exporting.
5. Run the export.
6. Check the final file after export completes.

## Tips
- Match export settings to the platform you plan to publish to.
- Review one exported sample before batch exporting multiple deliverables.

## Limitations
- Export time may vary based on output size and current workload.
