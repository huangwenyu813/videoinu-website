# Basic Workflow

## Summary
The standard Videoinu workflow is: define intent, choose a feature or model, generate, review, iterate, and export.

## When to use
Use this when you need a high-level process for most video creation tasks.

## Steps
1. Clarify the type of video you want to make.
2. Pick the right generation path, such as image-to-video or text-to-video.
3. Choose the model that fits your quality, speed, or style goal.
4. Generate an initial result.
5. Review continuity, quality, and composition.
6. Iterate until the output is ready.
7. Export the final version.

## Tips
- Start with a simple test output before scaling up.
- Change one variable at a time when troubleshooting quality.

## Limitations
- Different models may respond differently to the same input.
