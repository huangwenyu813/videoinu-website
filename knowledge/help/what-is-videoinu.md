# What Is Videoinu 2.0

## Summary
Videoinu 2.0 is an AI video collaboration platform designed to help creators move from idea to finished video with more control, flexibility, and multi-model support.

## When to use
Use Videoinu when you want to plan, generate, iterate, and export AI video inside one connected workflow.

## Steps
1. Start a new project.
2. Define your creative direction, script, or storyboard.
3. Choose the feature or model that fits your task.
4. Generate and refine your output.
5. Export the final result.

## Tips
- Think in workflow stages instead of one-shot generation.
- Use the platform to iterate on shots, continuity, and quality.

## Limitations
- Exact feature behavior depends on the currently available models and workflows in your workspace.
