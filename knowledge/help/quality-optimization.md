# Quality Optimization

## Summary
Quality improves when you tighten the creative goal, choose the right model, and iterate in smaller steps.

## When to use
Use this guide when the output looks inconsistent, weak, noisy, or far from the intended result.

## Steps
1. Clarify the exact problem: consistency, motion, style, framing, or detail.
2. Narrow the prompt or motion direction.
3. Adjust one setting at a time.
4. Try a more suitable model for the task.
5. Re-run a smaller test before scaling up.

## Tips
- Avoid changing prompt, model, and settings all at once.
- Continuity-sensitive work benefits from stable workflow choices.

## Limitations
- Higher quality often requires more iteration and slower workflows.
