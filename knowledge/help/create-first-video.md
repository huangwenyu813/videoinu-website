# Create Your First Video

## Summary
Your first video should be a simple, short test so you can understand the platform quickly.

## When to use
Use this tutorial when you are new to Videoinu and want the fastest path to a working result.

## Steps
1. Create a new project.
2. Choose a simple workflow, such as text-to-video or image-to-video.
3. Start with one short concept or one clear source image.
4. Choose a model and generate a first pass.
5. Review what worked and what did not.
6. Make one focused adjustment.
7. Export the best result after review.

## Tips
- Keep the first attempt small and controlled.
- Learn the effect of one setting change at a time.

## Limitations
- The first generation is often a draft, not the final polished version.
