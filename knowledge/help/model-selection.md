# Model Selection Guide

## Summary
Choose your model based on the task, the quality target, the need for consistency, and the generation speed you want.

## When to use
Use this guide when you are unsure which model to use for a project or step in the workflow.

## Steps
1. Define whether your task is ideation, short-form output, cinematic refinement, or continuity-heavy generation.
2. Decide whether speed or control matters more for this step.
3. Use a faster model for early exploration.
4. Use a stronger or more controlled model for final refinement.
5. Re-test if the result quality or consistency is not meeting your target.

## Tips
- Do early iterations with a faster workflow if speed matters.
- Use the same model during continuity-sensitive sequences when possible.

## Limitations
- Model availability may change over time.
- A model that works well for one style may not be ideal for another.
