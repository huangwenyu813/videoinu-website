# Text-to-Video

## Summary
Text-to-video lets you start from a written concept and generate motion output directly from prompt guidance.

## When to use
Use this workflow when you are ideating or when you do not yet have a source image.

## Steps
1. Open the text-to-video workflow or feature.
2. Write a focused prompt describing the desired scene or outcome.
3. Choose the model that fits the task.
4. Generate an initial output.
5. Review whether the result matches your intent.
6. Tighten the prompt or settings if needed.

## Tips
- Use short, specific prompts before expanding to longer creative instructions.
- Test multiple prompt variations instead of rewriting everything at once.

## Limitations
- Broad prompts can produce inconsistent outcomes.
